How It Works

From your first message to a vehicle arriving at your port — every step explained simply.

01

Search & Bidding

Send us the specifications of what you want by WhatsApp or email. What to include:

  • Vehicle model
  • Year range
  • Budget
  • Destination port
  • Other specific preferences (colour, grade, transmission)
02

Deposit

Pay your deposit (30% of the price of the vehicle) for bidding on your behalf. The deposit will be deducted from the final price after a successful purchase.

03

Win & Invoice

If you win, we issue your invoice including auction fees, inland transport, and shipping options.

04

Payment & Booking

Payment arrives, shipping is booked, and the export process starts.

06

Shipping

The ship will depart Japan based on the port schedule. Tracking information and regular updates will be shared with you throughout the voyage.

07

Documents

Export documents are prepared and sent to you ahead of arrival.

08

Arrival & Collection

The ship arrives at your port. Complete customs procedures and collect your vehicle.
