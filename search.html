Understanding Auction Grades

Japan uses a standardised grading system for all auctioned vehicles.

S

Excellent / Near New

Virtually no wear. Often low mileage or near-new dealer stock.

5

Excellent

Minimal scratches or blemishes. Interior and mechanical in excellent condition.

4.5

Very Good

Minor surface marks only. No structural damage. Our most common sourced grade.

4

Good

Small dents or scratches, easily repaired. Mechanically sound. Strong value grade.

3.5

Above Average

Visible minor damage. Fully functional. Good budget option for resale markets.

3

Average

Noticeable wear or minor repair needed. Sourced on specific request only.
